White Pizza

White pizza is a pie topped with ricotta seasoned with fresh oregano and thyme, mozzarella, pecorino Romano, garlic chips, and black pepper for a rich, cheesy flavor.

49 min 4.9 (248)
Dinner Intermediate

June Xie

Ingredients in this White Pizza Recipe

  1. Pizza Dough: One pound of dough, divided in half, forming the base of the white pizza, providing a soft, chewy, and flavorful crust.
  2. Vegetable Oil: Used for brushing the baking sheets to prevent sticking.
  3. Cornmeal: Coarse cornmeal for sprinkling on the baking sheets to give the crust texture.
  4. Olive Oil: ¬º cup extra-virgin olive oil, heated with garlic to create flavorful garlic oil.
  5. Garlic: 4 cloves, thinly sliced, used to infuse the oil and make crispy garlic chips.
  6. Ricotta: 1¬Ω cups, mixed with herbs to form a creamy topping.
  7. Kosher Salt: ¬Ω tsp, added to season the ricotta mixture.
  8. Black Pepper: ¬Ω tsp freshly ground, adds mild heat and seasoning to the ricotta.
  9. Oregano: 1 Tbsp freshly chopped, used in ricotta and as garnish.
  10. Thyme: 1 tsp freshly chopped, mixed into the ricotta for extra flavor.
  11. Mozzarella: 2 cups shredded, divided. Main cheesy layer on each pizza.
  12. Pecorino Romano: ¬º cup, divided. Adds sharp, salty flavor to complement the mozzarella.
