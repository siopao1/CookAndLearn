Okonomiyaki

Okonomiyaki is a Japanese teppanyaki savory pancake dish consisting of wheat flour batter and other ingredients cooked on a teppan.

35 min 4.7 (174)
Lunch Beginner

Namiko Hirasawa Chen

Ingredients in this Okonomiyaki Recipe

  1. All-Purpose Flour: Serves as the main ingredient for the batter, providing structure, texture, and a smooth, consistent base for Takoyaki.
  2. Salt: Balances flavors in the batter.
  3. Sugar: Adds a touch of sweetness to the batter.
  4. Baking Powder: Helps the batter rise and become fluffy.
  5. Grated Mountain Yam (Nagaimo/Yamaimo): Adds stickiness and a light, airy texture to the batter.
  6. Dashi (Japanese Soup Stock): Provides rich umami flavor.
  7. Eggs: Binds the batter and adds richness (JUST Egg can be used for vegan).
  8. Tenkasu (Tempura Scraps): Adds crunch and extra texture.
  9. Pickled Red Ginger (Kizami Shoga): Adds a tangy and slightly spicy flavor.
  10. Green Cabbage: Finely chopped; the main vegetable in the pancake.
  11. Sliced Pork Belly: Classic okonomiyaki topping that adds savory flavor.
