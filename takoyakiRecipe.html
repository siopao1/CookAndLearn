Takoyaki

Is a popular Japanese street food consisting of small, round balls made from a wheat flourbased batter filled with diced octopus, pickled ginger, and green onions.

25 min 4.5 (269)
Dinner Intermediate

Namiko Hirasawa Chen

Ingredients in this Takoyaki Recipe

  1. Boiled Octopus: Tender octopus, cut into bite-sized pieces, serving as the main protein in Takoyaki, adding chewy texture and flavor.
  2. Katsuobushi: Dried bonito flakes that add smoky, umami flavor.
  3. Red Pickled Ginger (Beni Shoga): Adds a tangy, slightly spicy flavor.
  4. Tenkasu: Tempura scraps that provide crunch and texture.
  5. Green Onion / Scallion: Finely sliced; adds a fresh, mild onion flavor.
  6. Neutral Oil: Used to grease the takoyaki pan.
  7. Batter Ingredients:
  8. All-Purpose Flour: Forms the base structure of the batter.
  9. Baking Powder: Makes the batter light and fluffy.
  10. Eggs: Helps bind the batter together.
  11. Dashi: Japanese soup stock that adds deep umami flavor.
  12. Soy Sauce: Provides savory seasoning.
  13. Kosher Salt: Enhances overall flavor.
