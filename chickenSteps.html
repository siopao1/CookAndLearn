Chicken Parmesan

Chicken parmesan features crispy breaded chicken topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella and Parmesan.


50 min 4.7 (92)
Dinner Beginner

Alejandro Valdes Lora

Step-by-Step Guide: Chicken Parmesan

  1. Make the Marinara Sauce: Saut√© onion and garlic in oil, add tomatoes and basil, simmer 30 minutes, season, and discard basil.
  2. Prepare the Chicken: Dredge chicken in flour, dip in egg, coat with panko, garlic powder, and Parmesan.
  3. Assemble Cheese Mixture: Mix mozzarella with remaining Parmesan.

  4. Cook the Chicken: Fry chicken in oil until golden, top with marinara and cheese mixture.

  5. Broil the Chicken: Broil until cheese is melted and lightly browned, about 2 minutes.

  6. Serve: Plate chicken, top with basil, and serve with remaining marinara.
