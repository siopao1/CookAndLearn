Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a Thai stir-fried dish made with flat rice noodles, egg, shrimp or tofu, bean sprouts, and chives, flavored with tamarind, fish sauce, and sugar, and topped with lime and crushed peanuts.

20 min 4.6 (92)
Dinner Intermediate

Sherri Pengjad

Pad Thai Recipe

  1. Rice noodles: 16 oz. fresh rice noodles. Fresh noodles are softer and give a more authentic Pad Thai texture.
  2. Tamarind: Half a tamarind block. Soften and strain to extract the paste for the signature tangy Pad Thai sauce.
