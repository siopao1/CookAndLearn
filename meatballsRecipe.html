Italian Meatballs

Classic Italian meatballs are tender beef-pork-sausage balls made moist with a bread-milk panade, seared crispy, then simmered in marinara sauce for rich flavor.

45 min 4.8 (88)
Dinner Beginner

Lauren Miyashiro

Ingredients in this Italian Meatballs Recipe

  1. Bread crumbs: One cup of fresh crumbs, adding structure, lightness, and a tender texture to the meatballs while helping them hold shape.
  2. Milk: 1/2 cup whole. Soaks the bread crumbs for moisture.
  3. Ground beef: 1 lb. Adds rich, hearty flavor.
  4. Ground pork: 1/2 lb. Adds tenderness and depth of flavor.
  5. Italian sausage: 1/2 lb., casings removed. Adds spice and savory flavor.
  6. Yellow onion: 1 small, finely chopped. Adds sweetness and aroma.
  7. Garlic: 3 cloves, finely chopped. Adds fragrance and flavor.
  8. Kosher salt: To season the meatballs.
  9. Black pepper: Freshly ground. Adds mild heat and flavor.
  10. Eggs: 2 large, beaten. Binds the meat mixture.
  11. Parmesan: 1 cup finely grated, plus more for serving. Adds savory, cheesy flavor.
  12. Parsley: 1/4 cup chopped, plus more for serving. Adds fresh herb flavor.
  13. Extra-virgin olive oil: 2 Tbsp. Used for searing meatballs.
  14. Marinara sauce: 1 (32-oz.) jar or homemade. Provides the sauce for simmering meatballs.
