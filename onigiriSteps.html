Onigiri

Onigiri, also known as omusubi or nigirimeshi, is a Japanese rice ball made from white rice


45 min 4.9 (78)
Dinner Beginner

Namiko Hirasawa Chen

Step-by-Step Guide: Onigiri

  1. Cook the Rice: Use short-grain rice and cook it using a rice cooker, stovetop pot, Instant Pot, or donabe.
  2. Shape the Rice Balls: Form the cooked rice into balls or triangles using a mold or your hands. Season with salt and add optional fillings if desired.
  3. Wrap with Nori: Cover each rice ball with a strip or sheet of nori before serving.
