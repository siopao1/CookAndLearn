Massaman Curry

Massaman curry is a rich, mild Thai curry with Muslim influences, made with chicken or beef, coconut milk, potatoes, onions, peanuts, and warm spices.

60 min 4.8 (95)
Dinner Intermediate

Sherri Pengjad

Step-by-Step Guide: Massaman Curry

  1. Heat the Oil: Add cooking oil to a large pot over medium heat.

  2. Cook the Curry Paste: Add Massaman curry paste and stir for a few minutes until fragrant and the oil separates.
  3. Add Coconut Cream: Spoon out the thick cream from the coconut milk and add it to the pot. Stir and heat until bubbly.
  4. Add Chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the pot and mix with the curry paste and coconut cream.
  5. Add the Remaining Coconut Milk and Water: Pour in the rest of the coconut milk and enough water to cover the chicken. Bring to a simmer and cook 10‚Äì15 minutes until the chicken is partially cooked.
  6. Add Potatoes and Onions: Add potatoes and onions. Continue simmering until the potatoes are tender and the chicken is fully cooked.
  7. Add Peanuts: Stir in roasted peanuts for added crunch and texture.

  8. Season: Adjust the curry with tamarind paste, fish sauce, and sugar to taste.

  9. Finish and Serve: Sprinkle cilantro on top and serve hot with steamed jasmine rice.
