Chicken Empanadas

Chicken empanadas are savory pastries filled with shredded chicken, vegetables, and spices, baked until golden.

60 min 4.9 (156)
Lunch Intermediate

Justin Sullivan

Ingredients in this Chicken Empanadas Recipe

  1. Chicken Thighs: 2 lb., boneless and skinless. Main protein for the filling.
  2. Salt: To taste. Enhances the flavor of chicken and vegetables.
  3. Black Pepper: Freshly ground, to taste. Adds mild heat and depth.
  4. Bay Leaves: 2 leaves. Adds subtle aromatic flavor.
  5. Vegetable Oil: 2 Tbsp., for saut√©ing aromatics and chicken.
  6. Onion: 1 large, chopped. Adds sweetness and depth.
  7. Red Bell Pepper: 1, chopped. Adds color and mild sweetness.
  8. Orange Bell Pepper: 1, chopped. Adds color and mild sweetness.
  9. Garlic: 3 cloves, minced. Adds aromatic flavor.
  10. Tomato Paste: 2 Tbsp. Adds richness and umami.
  11. Sweet Paprika: 1 Tbsp. Adds smoky-sweet flavor and color.
  12. Cumin: 2 tsp. Adds warm, earthy notes.
  13. Olives: 1/2 cup Castelvetrano or green, roughly chopped. Adds briny, savory flavor.
