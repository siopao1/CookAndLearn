Tonkatsu

Made of a thick slice of pork, typically loin or tenderloin, that is coated in flour, egg, and panko (Japanese breadcrumbs), and then deep-fried until golden and crispy

30 min 4.8 (188)
Lunch Intermediate

Namiko Hirasawa Chen

Ingredients in this Tonkatsu Recipe

  1. Pork Loin or Pork Tenderloin Fillet: Contains a layer of fat for extra tenderness. Main protein for tonkatsu.
  2. Salt and Black Pepper: Season the pork for flavor.
  3. All-Purpose Flour: Used to coat pork before egg and panko.
  4. Large Eggs: Binds the panko to the pork.
  5. Panko Breadcrumbs: Provides a crispy coating for the fried pork.
  6. Neutral Oil (Vegetable Oil): For deep-frying the pork.
  7. Tonkatsu Sauce: Sweet-savory sauce for dipping.
  8. Black and White Sesame Seeds: Optional, for grinding into the dipping sauce.
