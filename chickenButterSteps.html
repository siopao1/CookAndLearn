Butter Chicken

Butter chicken features tender tandoori-style chicken simmered in a creamy, spiced tomato and butter sauce.

60 min 4.8 (186)
Dinner Beginner

Mitchman

Step-by-Step Guide: Indian Butter Chicken

  1. Initial Marination: Coat chicken with lemon juice, chili powder, and salt. Mix well, cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  2. Prepare Yogurt Mix: Drain yogurt briefly. Combine with lemon juice, olive oil, garlic paste, ginger paste, melted butter, chili powder, and garam masala.
  3. Marinate Chicken: Pour yogurt mixture over chicken. Mix evenly. Cover and refrigerate 3‚Äì4 hours for deeper flavor.
  4. Cook Chicken: Preheat oven to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C). Thread chicken onto skewers and bake for about 20 minutes until nearly cooked.
  5. Make Sauce: Melt butter. Add garam masala, ginger paste, garlic, and green chile. Stir in tomato puree, water, chili powder, salt, honey, and fenugreek. Simmer gently.
  6. Combine and Finish: Add cooked chicken to sauce. Simmer until tender. Stir in heavy cream. Serve with rice or naan.
