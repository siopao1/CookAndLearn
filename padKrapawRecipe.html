Pad Krapaw

Pad Krapaw is a quick Thai stir-fry of spicy ground meat with garlic, chilies, and holy basil, commonly served with rice and crispy fried eggs.

15 min 4.8 (124)
Lunch Beginner

Sherri Pengjad

Ingredients in this Pad Krapaw Recipe

  1. Cooking oil: 1 tablespoon. For stir-frying aromatics and meat.

  2. Garlic: 5 cloves, smashed. Adds fragrant flavor.

  3. Thai fresh chilies: 2‚Äì15, depending on your spice preference. Adjust heat to taste.

  4. Ground meat: 1.5 pounds, chicken, beef, pork, or turkey. Main protein for the stir-fry.

  5. Green beans (optional): 1/2 cup, chopped into 1/2-inch pieces. Adds crunch and texture.

  6. Fish sauce: 2 tablespoons, to taste. Adds umami and saltiness.

  7. Black soy sauce: 1 teaspoon. Adds color and mild sweetness.

  8. Sugar: 1 teaspoon. Balances salty and spicy flavors.

  9. Holy basil leaves and flowers: 1 cup. Use Thai holy basil for authentic flavor, or substitute with sweet basil.
