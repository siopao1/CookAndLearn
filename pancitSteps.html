Pancit

Quick and easy pancit is a Filipino noodle stir-fry featuring rice noodles tossed with chicken, pork, shrimp, cabbage, carrots, soy sauce, and garlic for a savory one-pan meal

40 min 4.8 (92)
Breakfast Beginner

Heather Maurer

Step-by-Step Guide: Pancit

  1. Prep the Noodles. Place rice noodles in a bowl, cover with warm water, and let soften for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Saut√© Aromatics. Heat oil in a wok over medium-low heat. Add onion and garlic; cook until the onion is tender.
  3. Cook the Meat and Veggies. Stir in the chicken, cabbage, carrots, and soy sauce. Cook until the cabbage begins to soften.
  4. Add the Noodles. Toss in the softened noodles and cook, stirring constantly, until everything is heated through.
  5. Serve. Transfer pancit to a serving dish and garnish with lemon wedges.
