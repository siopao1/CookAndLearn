Chili Verde

Chile verde is a Northern Mexican pork stew with tender pork braised in a tangy green tomatillo and chili sauce.

145 min 4.6 (123)
Dinner Intermediate

Laura Manzano

Step-by-step Guide: Chili Verde

  1. Roast Vegetables: Preheat broiler to high. Arrange tomatillos, poblanos, cubanelles, serranos, onion, and garlic on a baking sheet. Broil 10‚Äì15 minutes, flipping tomatillos and peppers halfway, until deeply charred. Let cool.
  2. Brown Pork: Heat the Dutch oven over medium-high. Season pork with oil, salt, and pepper. Add to the pot in an even layer. Cook undisturbed 5 minutes, stir and flatten, cook 5 more minutes, then stir occasionally until browned all over, about 5 minutes more.
  3. Add Spices: Stir in cumin, oregano, and coriander. Cook 30 seconds until fragrant.

  4. Blend Sauce: In a blender, combine roasted vegetables, chicken broth, and 1 tablespoon salt. Blend until smooth. Pour into the pot with pork. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low.
  5. Simmer Covered: Cook covered, stirring occasionally, about 1¬Ω hours, until pork is tender and sauce is thickened.
  6. Reduce the Sauce: Remove lid and cook uncovered 45 minutes, stirring frequently, until sauce is thickened. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
