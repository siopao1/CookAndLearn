Chicken Empanadas

Chicken empanadas are savory pastries filled with shredded chicken, vegetables, and spices, baked until golden.

60 min 4.9 (156)
Lunch Intermediate

Justin Sullivan

Step-by-step Guide: Chicken Empanadas

  1. Poach Chicken: Pat chicken thighs dry and season with salt and pepper. Place in a pot, cover with water plus 1 tablespoon salt and bay leaves; bring to a boil. Reduce to medium-low, cover, and cook until chicken reaches 165¬∫F, about 10 minutes. Shred chicken and reserve 1 cup cooking liquid.
  2. Saut√© Vegetables: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high until shimmering. Add chopped onion and bell peppers; cook until onion is golden and peppers brown on edges, 6 to 8 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Build Filling: Stir in tomato paste and cook until darkened, 2 to 3 minutes. Add shredded chicken, paprika, cumin, and oregano; cook 4 to 5 minutes until heated and spices toast. Stir in chopped olives and reserved cooking liquid; cook until glossy sauce forms, 2 to 3 minutes. Cool slightly.
  4. Assemble Empanadas: Preheat oven to 375¬∫F. Roll each empanada wrapper slightly larger. Place 2 to 3 tablespoons filling in center, brush edges with beaten egg, fold into half-moon, and crimp edges securely.
  5. Bake: Arrange on a baking sheet, brush tops with egg wash. Bake until golden brown, 20 to 25 minutes. Cool 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
