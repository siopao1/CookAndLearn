Tamales

Tamales are Mexican steamed masa packets filled with meats like chicken or pork, wrapped in corn husks or parchment.

180 min 4.8 (92)
Breakfast Advanced

Stacey Ballis

Ingredients in this Tamales Recipe

  1. Masa Harina (for tamales): Dried, 2 cups. Coarser grind than tortilla masa, gives light, fluffy texture.
  2. Kosher Salt: 1 teaspoon. Enhances flavor without over-salting.
  3. Shortening or Lard: 1/2 cup. Whipped to aerate dough; lard adds traditional savory flavor, shortening works for vegetarian option.
  4. Baking Powder: 1 teaspoon. Ensures light, cohesive dough.
  5. Broth (low-sodium chicken or vegetable): 1 cup. Hydrates masa and adds umami flavor.
  6. Wrapping: Parchment squares or corn husks. Corn husks are traditional; parchment works as substitute.
