Thai Kao Pad

Thai Khao Pad is a quick, flavorful fried rice made with jasmine rice, eggs, garlic, vegetables, and chicken or other proteins, seasoned with fish and soy sauce.

15 min 4.7 (211)
Breakfast Beginner

Sherri Pengjad

Step-by-Step Guide: Thai Kao Pad

  1. Prepare the stir-fry sauce: Mix fish sauce (or light soy), oyster/mushroom soy, dark soy (if using), and sugar in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Cook aromatics: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok. Stir-fry the diced onion and chopped garlic until soft and fragrant.
  3. Scramble eggs and cook chicken & veggies: Push aromatics to the side, add a little more oil if needed, pour in beaten eggs and scramble gently. Add chicken and stir-fry until almost cooked; toss in vegetables and cook together until crisp-tender.
  4. Set aside: Transfer the chicken, eggs, and vegetable mixture to a plate.

  5. Fry the rice: Add 1 tbsp oil to the wok, break up the day-old rice and stir-fry until heated through and slightly crisp.
  6. Combine everything: Add the prepared stir-fry sauce to the rice, return the chicken and vegetable mixture to the wok, and toss until evenly coated and heated.
  7. Finish and garnish: Turn off the heat, stir in green onions and white pepper. Serve with cilantro, lime wedges, cucumber slices, or Nam Pla Prik as desired.
