Chicken Parmesan

Chicken parmesan features crispy breaded chicken topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella and Parmesan.


50 min 4.7 (92)
Dinner Beginner

Alejandro Valdes Lora

Ingredients in this Chicken Parmesan Recipe

  1. Extra-virgin olive oil: Three tablespoons, used to saut√© onions and garlic, enhancing their aroma and adding rich, flavorful depth to the dish.
  2. Yellow onion: 1 small, finely chopped. Adds sweetness and flavor.
  3. Garlic: 6 cloves, finely chopped. Adds aroma and depth.
  4. Crushed tomatoes: 1 (28-oz.) can. Forms the base of the sauce.
  5. Basil: 2 large sprigs. Adds fresh herb flavor.
  6. Kosher salt: To season the sauce.
  7. Black pepper: Freshly ground. Adds mild heat and flavor.
  8. Panko bread crumbs: 1 cup. Provides a crispy coating.
  9. Parmesan: 2 cups, finely grated and divided. Adds savory, cheesy flavor.
  10. Flour: 3/4 cup all-purpose. Helps breading adhere.
  11. Eggs: 2 large. Binds the coating to the chicken.
  12. Chicken cutlets: 6 (1/4"-thick), boneless, skinless (about 1 1/2 lb. total). Main protein of the dish.
  13. Mozzarella: 1 cup, shredded. Adds melty, creamy texture.
  14. Basil: Chopped, for serving. Adds color and fresh flavor.
