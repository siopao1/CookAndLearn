Pancit

Quick and easy pancit is a Filipino noodle stir-fry featuring rice noodles tossed with chicken, pork, shrimp, cabbage, carrots, soy sauce, and garlic for a savory one-pan meal.

40 min 4.8 (92)
Breakfast Beginner

Heather Maurer

Ingredients in this Pancit Recipe

  1. Rice noodles: One 12-ounce package, serving as the main starch of the dish, providing a hearty base and absorbing the flavors of the sauce.
  2. Vegetable oil: 1 tsp. Used for saut√©ing the aromatics.
  3. Onion: 1 small, finely diced. Adds flavor and aroma.
  4. Garlic: 3 cloves, minced. Enhances the savory flavor.
  5. Cooked chicken breast: 2 cups, diced. Main protein source.
  6. Cabbage: 1 small head, thinly sliced. Adds volume, texture, and mild sweetness.
  7. Carrots: 4 pieces, thinly sliced. Adds color, crunch, and natural sweetness.
  8. Soy sauce: 1/4 cup. Provides seasoning and umami flavor.
  9. Calamansi: 2, cut into wedges. Used for garnish and added brightness when serving.
