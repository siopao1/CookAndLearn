Beef Stir-Fry

Beef Stir Fry is always a surefire success at the dining table. Simple but delicious, stir fry‚Äôs combination of soy sauce and oyster sauce is fragrant and can easily make your mouth water.

Step-by-Step Guide: Beef Stir-Fry

  1. Marinate the beef: Combine beef, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and ground black pepper in a bowl. Mix well and let it stand for 5 minutes.

  2. Add cornstarch: Sprinkle cornstarch over the marinated beef and mix until evenly coated. Set aside.

  3. Cook the bell peppers: Heat oil in a pan and fry the bell peppers for 1 minute, stirring occasionally. Remove from the pan and set aside.

  4. Stir-fry the beef: Using the remaining oil in the pan, stir-fry the marinated beef over high heat until it begins to brown.

  5. Combine ingredients: Add the fried bell peppers back into the pan and toss until everything is well combined and heated through.

  6. Serve: Transfer the beef stir-fry to a serving plate. Serve with rice. Share and enjoy!
