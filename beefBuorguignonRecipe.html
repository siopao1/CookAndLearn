Beef Buorguignon

Beef Bourguignon is a classic French stew made with tender beef slow-cooked in red wine, onions, garlic, and herbs, resulting in deep, rich flavors.

195 min 4.4 (228)
Dinner Advance

Julia Child

Ingredients in this Beef Buorguignon Recipe

  1. Beef:Use high-quality beef, such as beef chuck or stewing beef, cut into large, uniform cubes to ensure even cooking and tender, flavorful results during the slow braising process.
  2. Red wine: Burgundy wine is traditional, giving the stew deep flavor and color.
  3. Beef broth: Adds richness and enhances the overall savory taste of the dish.
  4. Bacon or pancetta: Provides smoky flavor and extra depth when browned.
  5. Carrots: Cut into thick slices to add sweetness and texture.
  6. Onions: Pearl onions or regular chopped onions add aromatic flavor.
  7. Garlic: Minced or crushed to add fragrance and savory notes.
  8. Mushrooms: Sliced and saut√©ed for earthy flavor and added texture.
  9. Tomato paste: Helps thicken the sauce and adds richness.
  10. Flour: Used to coat the beef lightly and help create a thick, velvety sauce.
  11. Butter or oil: For browning the meat and saut√©ing vegetables.
  12. Fresh thyme: Adds herby aroma and classic French flavor.
  13. Bay leaves: Simmered in the stew to deepen flavor.
  14. Salt and pepper: Essential for seasoning the dish to taste.
  15. Parsley (optional): Chopped and sprinkled on top before serving.
