Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken tikka masala features grilled spiced chicken simmered in a creamy tomato sauce with garlic and cream.

100 min 4.7 (126)
Lunch Intermediate

Yakuta

Step-by-Step Guide: Chicken Tikka Masala

  1. Prepare Marinade: Mix yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, cinnamon, cayenne, pepper, ginger, and salt in a large bowl until completely smooth and blended.
  2. Marinate Chicken: Add chicken pieces to the mixture. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour to absorb flavors.
  3. Preheat Grill: Heat grill to high. Lightly oil the grate to prevent sticking during cooking.
  4. Grill Chicken: Thread chicken onto skewers, discard marinade, and grill until juices run clear, about 5 minutes per side.
  5. Prepare Sauce: Melt butter in a skillet. Saut√© garlic and jalape√±o. Add cumin, paprika, salt, tomato sauce, and cream. Simmer 20 minutes until thickened.
  6. Combine Chicken and Sauce: Add grilled chicken to the sauce. Simmer 10 minutes to blend flavors.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Transfer to a serving dish. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot over rice or with naan.
