Marry Me Chicken

Marry Me Chicken is a rich, flavorful dish of chicken breasts baked in a creamy garlic-Parmesan sauce with sun-dried tomatoes, thyme, and a touch of red pepper flakes.

30 min 4.6 (192)
Dinner Beginner

Lindsay Funston

Ingredients in this Marry Me Chicken Recipe

  1. Extra-virgin olive oil: Three tablespoons, divided, used for searing chicken and saut√©ing aromatics, adding rich flavor and a smooth, fragrant finish.
  2. Chicken breasts: 4 boneless, skinless. Main protein of the dish.
  3. Kosher salt: To season the chicken and sauce.
  4. Black pepper: Freshly ground. Adds mild heat and flavor.
  5. Garlic: 2 cloves, finely chopped. Adds aroma and depth.
  6. Thyme: 1 Tbsp. fresh leaves. Adds herbal flavor.
  7. Red pepper flakes: 1 tsp. Adds mild heat.
  8. Chicken broth: 3/4 cup, low-sodium. Forms the base of the sauce.
  9. Sun-dried tomatoes: 1/2 cup, finely chopped. Adds concentrated tomato flavor.
  10. Heavy cream: 1/2 cup. Adds richness and creaminess.
  11. Parmesan: 1/4 cup, finely grated. Adds savory, cheesy flavor.
  12. Basil: Torn fresh, for serving. Adds color and fresh flavor.
