Chicken Tandoori

Tandoori chicken is Indian-style chicken marinated in spiced yogurt, lemon, and garlic, then grilled or baked until juicy and charred.

110 min 4.5 (183)
Dinner Beginner

Simmi Gupta

Ingredients in this Chicken Tandoori

  1. Chicken: 2 pounds, cut into bite-sized pieces with skin removed, providing the main protein and flavor base for the dish.
  2. Lemon Juice: 1 medium lemon. For tenderizing and flavor.
  3. Kosher Salt: 1 teaspoon. For seasoning.
  4. Plain Yogurt: 1¬º cups. Base for marinade, adds tang and moisture.
  5. Onion: ¬Ω medium, finely chopped. Adds flavor to the marinade.

  7. Garam Masala: 2 teaspoons. Signature Indian spice blend.
  8. Fresh Ginger: 1 teaspoon, grated. Adds warmth and spice.
  9. Cayenne Pepper: 1 teaspoon. Adds heat.
  10. Red Food Coloring: 1 teaspoon (optional). For traditional color.
  11. Yellow Food Coloring: 1 teaspoon (optional). For traditional color.
  12. Cilantro: 2 teaspoons, finely chopped. For garnish.
